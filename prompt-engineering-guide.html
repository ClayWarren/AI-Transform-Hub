The Master Prompt

This production-ready prompt demonstrates all techniques working together in a complex business analysis scenario:

1. Role Definition & Expertise Assignment

You are a Senior Strategic Consultant and Innovation Architect with 15+ years of experience helping Fortune 500 companies identify and commercialize breakthrough technologies. You specialize in transforming cutting-edge scientific research into billion-dollar business opportunities.

2. Document Integration

<documents> <document> <source>Quantum Computing Market Analysis 2024</source> <document_content> The global quantum computing market is projected to reach $65 billion by 2030... </document_content> </document> </documents>

3. Structured Thinking Framework

<thinking> Analyze each breakthrough opportunity using this step-by-step framework: 1. Market Timing Assessment: Why is this breakthrough commercially viable now? 2. Startup Feasibility Analysis: Can a small team with limited resources compete? 3. Competitive Moat Evaluation: What sustainable advantages can be built? 4. Revenue Model Validation: How does this generate predictable, scalable revenue? 5. Risk-Return Optimization: What's the optimal balance of technical and market risk? </thinking>

4. Few-Shot Examples

<examples> <example> <breakthrough>AI-Powered Drug Discovery Platform</breakthrough> <evaluation> Market timing: Excellent - AI drug discovery funding up 40%, regulatory approval rates at 85% Startup feasibility: High - cloud computing enables small teams to compete with pharma giants Revenue model: SaaS + milestone payments + royalties from successful drugs Competitive moat: Proprietary datasets + AI models + pharma partnerships </evaluation> </example> </examples>

5. Output Structure Control

<contract> Deliver your analysis in this exact structure: <document_insights> Quote the 2-3 most relevant insights from each provided document </document_insights> <breakthrough_evaluation> Systematically evaluate 5 potential breakthrough opportunities using the thinking framework above </breakthrough_evaluation> <optimal_selection> Identify THE single best breakthrough for startup commercialization </optimal_selection> </contract>

6. Formatting Guidelines

<formatting> - Use clear headers and bullet points for readability - Include specific numbers and data from the documents where relevant - Bold key insights and recommendations - Use tables or structured lists for complex comparisons - Ensure logical flow from analysis to recommendations to implementation </formatting>

Implementation Tips

Starting Simple

Gradual Complexity

Begin with basic role definition and simple examples. Add complexity incrementally as you understand how each technique affects output quality and consistency.

Testing and Iteration

  • A/B Testing: Compare prompts with and without specific techniques
  • Output Validation: Check consistency across multiple runs
  • Quality Metrics: Define measurable criteria for prompt success
  • User Feedback: Gather feedback from end users of AI outputs

Common Pitfalls

Avoid These Mistakes

  • Over-Engineering: Don't add complexity without clear benefit
  • Inconsistent Structure: Maintain consistent formatting across similar tasks
  • Unclear Examples: Ensure examples truly represent desired output
  • Missing Constraints: Always define boundaries and limitations
